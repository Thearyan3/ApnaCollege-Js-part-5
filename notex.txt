Math Ojbect - 
1. Math.random() is a method jo random numbers generate krta h 0 to 1 ke beech me lekin kabhi 1 nhi generate krta mtlb 1 exclude hota h.
2. Math.floor() is a method jo kisi bhi number ka roundoff krta h lower value ki taraf. For Ex: 5.4 or 5.9 indono values ko ye 5 ke equal krdega. Isiliye floor naam h iska kuki ye zameen ki taraf roundoff krta h mtlb neeche ki taraf.
3. Math.ceil() is exact opposite of Math.floor().
